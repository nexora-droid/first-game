# A Tale O' Lost Lovers

Explore three levels of fun, engaging and exciting gameplay and try to find your lost love.
She eagerly awaits you.

## How to Play?
`A, D, and Arrow Keys` keys to move left and right.
`W or Up Arrow` to jump.
`Shift` to open Spellbook in level 3.
